# Spending Tracker

Real-time spending logger with gamification. Track impulse levels, flag spending as aligned or avoidance, and watch savings goals fill up in real-time.

## Features

- **Real-time logging** — Log spending as it happens (amount, category, impulse level, optional note)
- **Impulse tracking** — Categorize each spend as Low, Medium, or High impulse
- **Smart flags** — Mark spending as Aligned (good choices) or Avoidance (gap-filling)
- **Gamified quests** — 7 savings goals that progress with aligned spending
- **Today's dashboard** — See impulse breakdown, category split, and aligned vs. avoidance totals
- **Local storage** — All data persists on your device, survives app updates
- **Export/import** — Backup your spending data anytime

## How It Works

**Log tab:** Quick real-time entry — amount, category, impulse level, optional note.

**Today tab:** Dashboard showing total spent, impulse breakdown (Low/Medium/High), spending by category, and aligned vs. avoidance split.

**Quests tab:** 7 savings goals (Cocktail class, Bouldering, Salsa, Music course, Skateboard gear, Travel fund, BJJ). Progress bars fill as you spend aligned money. Unlock them all.

**Settings tab:** Export spending data as JSON, or import from a previous backup.

## Install as PWA

**iPhone:**
1. Open in Safari
2. Tap Share (bottom center)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Name it "Spending Tracker"
5. Tap Add

**Android:**
1. Open in Chrome
2. Tap menu (three dots, top right)
3. Tap "Install app"
4. Confirm

The app works offline and saves everything locally.

## Why This Matters

Most people don't notice their spending because they're too busy. If you have the time and awareness to track it, you can see the patterns — impulse vs. intentional, avoidance vs. aligned. The gamification is intentional: use dopamine mechanics to reward good choices so your brain learns to prefer them.
